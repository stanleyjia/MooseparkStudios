About

On a mission to change the mobile game market

  • 2017

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Brainstorming and prototyping of Rainbow Drift starts in the summer of 2017

  • Spring 2018

    Initial Stages Completed

    Initial development stages are completed. Most of the basic programming is done at this point. Joseph Merkadeau joins on to help with Game Design and 3D modeling. Kyle joins to help with Audio Design.

  • Summer 2018

    Company Founded

    In the midst of balancing multiple internships, Stanley Jia and Joseph Merkadeau found the company, Moosepark Studios Inc. to further faciliate the development of Rainbow Drift. Catherine joins on to help with graphic design.

  • Fall 2018

    Game Released

    After months of development, debugging, and polishing, the game is finally released in September of 2018.
